Why Reforestation Matters

🌱

Fights Climate Change

Forests absorb CO₂ and cool the planet. Planting trees helps tackle global warming by sequestering carbon and regulating temperatures.

💧

Restores Water Cycle

Trees regulate rainfall and protect watersheds, maintaining clean water flow and preventing soil erosion and desertification.

🐿️

Saves Wildlife

Forests are home to 80% of terrestrial biodiversity. Reforestation revives habitats and protects endangered species.
