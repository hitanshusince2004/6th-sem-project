Why Reforestation Matters

🌱 Fights Climate Change

Forests absorb CO₂ and cool the planet. Planting trees helps tackle global warming.

💧 Restores Water Cycle

Trees regulate rainfall and protect watersheds, maintaining clean water flow.

🐿️ Saves Wildlife

Forests are home to countless species. Reforestation revives their habitats.
